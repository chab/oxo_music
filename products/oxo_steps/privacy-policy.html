Privacy Policy for Oxo Steps

Last updated: July 7, 2026

Oxo Steps does not collect personal data in the app.

Questions? Contact contact@oxo-music.com.

1. Information We Collect

Oxo Steps does not collect personal data from users through the app. The app does not require account creation and does not include user tracking.

2. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share personal data with third parties because we do not collect personal data in the app.

3. Analytics and Tracking

The app does not use advertising SDKs and does not perform cross-app tracking.

4. Audio/MIDI Processing

Oxo Steps is a MIDI tool. MIDI and session data are processed locally on your device and are not transmitted to our servers by the app.

5. Third-Party Hosts and Services

Oxo Steps may run inside third-party host apps (for example, AUv3 hosts). Those host apps are operated by third parties and have their own privacy policies.

6. Contact

If you have any privacy-related questions, contact contact@oxo-music.com.
